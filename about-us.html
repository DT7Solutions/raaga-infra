About Us

Raaga Infra is a growing construction company that is located in Guntur, India. Carrying more than nine years of existence makes us a trusting enterprise, who will maintain your trust.

Our History

Our Story

Raaga Infra established in the year 2012., brings to you a wholesome, premium and sophisticated way of living. It is an address of pomp and splendor that you’d be proud to own. Recognized as a household name for being one of the best for quality construction and timely deliveries, Raaga Infra is evinced as an astute leader of the construction sector In Guntur, Vijayawada Andhra Pradesh.

At Raaga Infra, we think that houses are the most emotionally and financially significant investment that individuals make throughout their lives. We are very enthusiastic about our houses and feel a natural responsibility to make owning and living in them a source of joy and pride, and we have applied this concept to every square foot of the Eight Lakh square feet we have constructed so far.

Raaga Infra has experienced continuous growth over the last 9 years. We build with more than just concrete and steel. We’ve been partners in many great projects. Over the years, we have expanded our services and geographical areas. The next chapter starts with you.

Our Mission

Our mission is more than just words, they are how we strive to live every day. We are able to be a trusted partner to our customers because they set the expectation for conducting our business in responsible and sustainable ways.

Our Vision

An ambitious vision is what we are building a company that will unleash our full potential. To continually deliver excellent value & innovative construction solutions to meet our clients' requirements.
